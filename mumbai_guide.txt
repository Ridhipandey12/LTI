Mumbai Travel Guide: Ultimate 2025 Edition

Introduction
Mumbai, the bustling heart of India, is a city of dreams, Bollywood glamour, and street food delights. Formerly Bombay, it's home to 21 million people and offers a mix of colonial history, modern skyscrapers, and pristine beaches. Ideal for budget travelers like Rahul—plan for 3-5 days under 50k INR.

Getting There
Fly into Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (BOM). Budget flights from Delhi: IndiGo ~3k INR one-way. Trains: Rajdhani Express from Delhi (2k INR, 16 hours). Local transport: Uber/Ola (100-300 INR/ride), BEST buses (20 INR), or Mumbai Metro (50 INR/day pass).

Accommodation
Budget AC Hotels: Ibis Mumbai (4k INR/night, near airport, clean rooms with WiFi). Colaba Guesthouses (2k INR, central but basic). Luxury: Taj Mahal Palace (20k INR, heritage views). Tip: Book via Booking.com for deals—Rahul's 50k covers 3 nights at Ibis + extras.

Beaches and Outdoor Activities
Juhu Beach: Iconic sunset spot, free entry. AC beach lounges: 2k INR for shaded cabanas with drinks. Horse rides: 300 INR/30 mins. Crowded weekends—go early. Versova Beach: Cleaner, quieter; kite surfing lessons 1.5k INR. Marine Drive: "Queen's Necklace" promenade—free evening walks, but rainy season (June-Sep) floods possible.

Rainy Day Indoor Spots
Monsoon hits hard—pack umbrellas! Prince of Wales Museum (500 INR entry, AC halls with Mughal artifacts and ancient sculptures). Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya—world-class exhibits. Gateway of India: Iconic arch (free exterior), boat to Elephanta Caves (200 INR ferry, cave tickets 40 INR—dry indoors). Shopping: Crawford Market (bargain spices, 100-500 INR).

Food and Budget Tips
Street Eats: Vada Pav (50 INR—must-try!), Pav Bhaji (80 INR). AC Dining: Leopold Cafe (Colaba, 500 INR meal, famous from Shantaram book). Total Food Budget: 5k INR for 3 days (mix street + mid-range). Avoid tourist traps—use Zomato for reviews. Water: Bisleri bottles only (20 INR).

History and Culture
Founded 1661 by Portuguese, gifted to Britain. Bollywood Tours: Film City (1k INR guided, see sets). Temples: Siddhivinayak (free, Ganesh idol—crowded Fridays). Festivals: Ganesh Chaturthi (Aug-Sep)—vibrant processions, but plan around monsoons.

Safety and Alerts
Safe for solo travelers, but watch pickpockets in crowds. Women: Use women-only metro cars. Check NewsAPI for floods (e.g., 2025 monsoons delayed). Emergency: 100 police.

Itinerary for Rahul (Budget 50k, AC Pref)
Day 1: Arrive, Ibis check-in (4k), Juhu Beach sunset (free).
Day 2: Museum (500), Leopold lunch (500), Metro to Gateway (50).
Day 3: Bollywood tour (1k), Versova Beach (free), depart.
Total: ~25k (room 12k, food 5k, sights/transport 8k). Buffer for rain: Swap beach for caves.
